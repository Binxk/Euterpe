Euterpe

Euterpe is a four-piece, Gadigal-based post-punk and art rock band. Drawing inspiration from artists such as Black Country New Road, Wednesday, Mary in the Junkyard, and Unwound, their sound fluctuates between feelings of erraticism, intimacy, and sincerity. Such stylings are captured in their recent single, ‘´Αγνή Παρθένε (O Virgin Pure)’, which deals with themes of degradation and purity.
